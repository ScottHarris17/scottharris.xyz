## About

Computational neuroscientist with a particular affinity for vision science. Professionally building diagnostic technologies for early detection of neurologic diseases. Experienced across the full research stack—from wet-lab electrophysiology and psychophysics to building the software that collects, analyzes, and visualizes the data. Outside the lab, writes philosophical essays on consciousness and free will, and enjoys amateur astronomy, dogs, and riding around on a Vespa.
