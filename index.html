Scott C. Harris, PhD

Hi, I'm Scott - A computational neuroscientist with a particular affinity for vision science. Professionally, I am building diagnostic technologies for early detection of neurologic diseases like Alzheimer's and Parkinson's. In my spare time, I enjoy being an amateur astronomer, playing with dogs, and riding around on my Vespa. This site contains an archive of my professional and personal works. I built it entirely from scratch using mainly vanilla JS and some helpful Bootstrap features.
